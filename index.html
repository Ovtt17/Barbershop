About Alura Barbershop

Located in the heart of the city, the Alura Barbershop brings to the market what is best for your hair and beard. Founded in 2020, Alura Barbershop is already a highlight in the city and conquers new clients daily.

Our mission is: "Provide self-esteem and quality of life to our clients".

We offer experienced professionals who are constantly observing the changes and movement in the world of fashion, in order to offer our clients the latest trends. The service has a pattern of excellence and agility, guaranteeing quality and satisfaction of our customers.
